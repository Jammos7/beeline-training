About Me

Welcome to Beeline Training Ltd, offering positive, reward based dog training in and around Rugby, Warwickshire.

With over ten years experience working in the dog behaviour industry, Alice Burke is an enthusiastic, experienced and positive trainer offering 121 and group puppy and dog training classes.

Alice started working with dogs from a young age. Following graduating with a BSc degree in Zoology, with a specialism in animal behaviour, Alice worked as a veterinary nursing assistant in Nottingham before pursuing a full time behaviourist job at Battersea Dogs & Cats Home in London. After working as a intake assessor and behaviourist with Battersea for a number of years, this was followed by further years as an Assistance Dog Instructor with Medical Detection Dogs, specialising in scent detection and client support. Alice has trained all kinds of breeds and temperaments.

Beeline Training Ltd. are strong advocates for force-free training and believe positive methods are the only methods! We are committed to encouraging reward-based training and the development of a strong and positive relationship between dogs and owners. Regardless of a dog’s history, age or breed, there’s always room for training, and we can show you how both you and your dog can enjoy working together towards great behaviour. 121 training classes are tailored to each dog and owner partnership, so you can be assured to receive bespoke training and advice. Or bring another four legged friend along for a discounted group class.

Get in touch with Alice and Beeline Training Ltd. to see how we can help you and your dog today.
